Recent testing in connection with a lawsuit found various products produced by Kroger and sold through various subsidiary grocery stores in California have lead levels that far exceed safe exposure levels according to California Law.

The following Simple Truth products were found to have high lead content, which may be sold at FoodsCo, Food 4 Less, Ralphs, or Ralphs Fresh Fare:

  • Simple Truth Organic Yellow Cling Diced Peaches & Pears
  • Simple Truth Organic Frozen Berry Medley
  • Simple Truth Organic 100% Whole Wheat Spaghetti
  • Simple Truth Organic Raisin Bran
  • Simple Truth Organic Cinnamon Breakfast Cookies

Exposure to the heavy metal is believed by the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention to be linked to intellectual disabilities in children and infertility and neurological problems in adults. The CDC has identified "no safe blood lead level in children," noting that "even low levels of lead in blood have been shown to affect learning, ability to pay attention, and academic achievement."
